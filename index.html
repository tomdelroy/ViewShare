Earthquakes

Earthquakes are patterns of seismic activity beneath the earths crust resulting in tremors,
and faults/cracks along the earths surface, displacing portions of land resulting in deaths and collateral damage
earthquakes are in the top 6 most donated to because they happen all the time, even if you don't hear about them because they're too small,
or in a country where media is restricted in certain areas

Fires

Bushfires are common in Australia, and can destroy up to 150 properties at a time, such as in the recent yarloop fires.
they can be sparked by arsonists in the hot dry summer months of Australia, or natural elements such as lightning.
bushfire recovery and victim rehab is donated to alot because of the growing economy of Australia, and because bushfires are common
equipment to extinguish fires or other services to rehabilitate post-fire are becoming cheaper.

Famines

famine is a shortage or clearence of all food in a certain area, they are usually caused by climate patterns preventing the successful growth
of food. they are common in African countries because of the extremely hot climate.
they are common to donate to as many charities make television advertisements for children in africa suffering from famine.

Floods

Floods are natural disasters in which water overflows from a body of water due to monsoonal rains or other elements.
